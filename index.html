My games from GAME 601 - Game Prototyping at American University in Fall 2019

Game Modification: We Could All Use a Little Change
Heads or tails just got more interesting. Draft coins of different values and toss them onto your opponent’s field in order to get points. But be careful—heads you’ll get points, tails you lose points. How much will you risk in order to win?

(Midterm) Non-Digital Game: Atlantis - City of Scoundrels
Everyone knows the queen of your fair city is about to kick off this mortal coil. The question is, which one of her relatives will she choose to take the throne? Vie for the favor of the queen and the people in this card-based strategy and resource management game and crown yourself ruler of Atlantis in "Atlantis: City of Scoundrels". Make engines, sabotage your opponents, and gain influence to win. 2-4 players.
RULES
Interactive Fiction: Subject 49

The year is 3012 and everything's gone to hell in a handbag. Earth has been evacuated and you're one of the subterranean scientists trying to find any sign of viable life on the surface. There's a promising sample but...it's got teeth. Find your fate in this hyperlink text-based adventure.

Visual Novel: In the Cage

What happens to Nicolas Cage's iconic characters once he wraps a project? They all join him in his Scottish mansion, of course. But the happy, homogenous cast of characters isn't as rosy as they seem. Escape the clutches of a narcissistic madman in this visual novel that asks the question: how unhinged is Nicolas Cage?
PLAY
(Final) Digital Game: I'm Always Tired
This 2D side-scroller platform-based game is meant to represent the concept of spoon theory: actions and interactions throughout the day cost spoons, and people with depression spend a number of their spoons just battling their depression. Learn about spoon theory with a friendly ghost boi. Created with Unity.
